Transaction Status Meanings
  • Matched - Auto-matched with ≥90% confidence
  • Review - Partial match (50-89% confidence) - needs review
  • Manual Match - User confirmed match (100% confidence)
  • Unmatched - No suitable match found (<50% confidence)
  • Invalid IBAN - IBAN does not exist in bank accounts
  • Inactive Account - Bank account is not active
  • Chargeback - Reversal or disputed transaction
Pro Tips
  • Use the Explain button to see why a transaction was matched
  • Transactions with Invalid IBAN or Inactive Account cannot be matched
  • Filter by Bank Account to focus on specific connections
  • Confidence Score indicates match quality: 90%+ auto-matches
  • Manually matched transactions are preserved and not re-matched automatically
  • Check the component breakdown in explanations: Amount, IBAN, Reference, Name
